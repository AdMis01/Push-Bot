# PushBot

The components:
- Esp32 - 2 piece
- L298N - 2 pieces
- MT3608 2A Step-Up - 2 pieces
- DC motor 1:48 3V-6V (To Weak/Slow) - 2 pieces
- Li-ion 18650 - 2 pieces
- Joystick - 1 piece
- Switch ON-OFF KCD1/KCD11 - 2 piece
